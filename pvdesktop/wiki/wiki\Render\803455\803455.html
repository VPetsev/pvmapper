Project Description
PVDesktop is an application for designing and analyzing specific solar energy sites.

PVDesktop is the desktop extension of the PVMapper online application. The goal of the PVMapper application is to reduce the "soft costs" of designing solar energy plants. The online application is optimized for analyzing and selecting sites for solar energy, while the PVDesktop is designed for designing and analyzing a specific site.
